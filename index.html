Kenz Coffee House

Premium Yemeni Coffee - 100% Organic

The Treasure of Yemen

Founded by Abdulnaser Alnajjar and Moshtak Alamri, Kenz brings the ancient heritage of Yemeni coffee to Canton, Michigan. Using 100% organic beans sourced directly from the mountains of Yemen, we offer a cultural experience in every cup.
